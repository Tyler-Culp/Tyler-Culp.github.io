Awards


These are awards from my time as part of the cybersecurity team at UC Davis. Our team competed in two main competitions: The Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition (CPTC) and The Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC).

In CPTC, we role-played as a penetration testing firm working on a social media company's security, while in CCDC, we acted as IT department hires tasked with maintaining and securing company services while keeping a separate red team at bay.

Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition (CPTC) 2025

  • 2nd place at Stanford's Western Regional Competition
  • Qualified for the National Competition (top 12 best schools globally), held at Rochester Institute of Technology

Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) 2025

  • 8th place in Western Region Qualifiers (out of 34 total teams)
  • Rank TBD in Western Regionals (out of top 8 schools from qualifiers)

Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) 2024

  • 5th place in Western Region Qualifiers (out of 30 total teams)
  • 4th place in Western Regionals (out of top 8 schools from qualifiers)
