Extra Curriculars

Davis Cyber Security Club

Throughout my time at Davis, I have been a member of the university's cybersecurity club. We participate in CTF challenges as well as both virtual and in-person blue team and red team competitions. Notable competitions we have competed in include the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) and the Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition (CPTC), which are considered the highest-level collegiate blue team and red team competitions, respectively.

Being an active team member has given me the opportunity to work with industry-standard networks and services. These events have provided hands-on experience configuring and maintaining essential services such as databases, Docker containers, web services, and networking protocols like DNS, SSH, and RDP. Additionally, these competitions offer real-world experience, as participants take on roles simulating penetration testers or IT employees.
